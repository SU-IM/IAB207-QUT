About this event

One of the fastest-growing K-Pop groups IVE have announced their first global outing -
1ST WORLD TOUR 'SHOW WHAT I HAVE'.

The 24-date world tour will hit various cities across Southeast Asia, North America, Europe, the U.K., South America, Eastern Asia and Australia in 2024.

IVE formed in 2021 and grew to major success following the release of their first single, 'ELEVEN' which went on to win multiple awards, alongside their other title tracks.

This year, IVE released their first full-length album [I've IVE], selling more than 1.1 million copies in the first week.

The group kicked off their 1ST WORLD TOUR 'SHOW WHAT I HAVE' with two incredible sold-out shows last month in Seoul.
